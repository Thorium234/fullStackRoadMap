Thorium234

Thorium-234 is a radioactive isotope of thorium with a half-life of approximately 24.1 days. It is produced as a decay product of uranium-238 and is part of the uranium decay series. Thorium-234 decays into protactinium-234 through beta decay, emitting a beta particle in the process. Due to its relatively short half-life, thorium-234 is not commonly found in nature and is primarily of interest in nuclear science and research.
